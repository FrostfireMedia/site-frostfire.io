Frostfire Auctions

Shane Clark

Auction plugins have, up until now, been limited to chat based, or player stores. There hasn't been a proper auction plugin until now. Use ingame chest menus, or an in-browser auction page, the possibilities are endless.

Features

Easy auctioning by players using a creative GUI.

A web UI for easy auctioning.
