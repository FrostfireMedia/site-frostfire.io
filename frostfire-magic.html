Frostfire Magic

Shane Clark

Magic and Mysteries. The Frostfire Magic modpack is filled with many different mods to add a new force to the Minecraftian world. Built upon the legendary Thaumcraft, there are many new features even to the most seasoned Minecraft player.

New Biomes

A plethora of new biomes have been added to the game. From towering mountains, to quicksand filled bogs. From evil forests, to pixie filled paradises. New challenges await all across the world.

New Tools

With new tools, weapons, and materials, there are countless combinations and customizations to make the perfect item. From hammers, and hatchets, to cleavers, and cutlasses, there has never been this many options available to players. Whatever you style, there is something for you.

New Sounds

Ambience. A key part of many games that is missing from Minecraft. Matmos does a fantastic job of filling in the silence. Bird chirps, wind, and a more realistic rain create a more immersive world. Enjoy the sweet sounds of nature.

New Enemies

There are plenty of enemies to test your steel and spells against. Face off against wasps, ents, hellspiders, werewolves, and many more. A number of new bosses join the fray, including the Lich, the Hydra, and even Death himself. Good luck.

New Dimensions

Tired of being restricted to just three dimensions? With the huge Twilight Forest, the terrifying Outer Lands, and access to the dangerous Dimensional Doors, the possibilities are almost endless.

Magic

The bread and butter of this modpack, Thaumcraft is an amazingly indepth mod, augmented by the addition of 6 addons. New spells, abilities, and items fill the Thaumonomicon. Better bring your reading glasses.

Quests

Having a tough time getting started? The quest book will help get you started, as well as give rewards for reaching milestones. Still in need of a real challenge? Switch to the hardcore quest mode, and see how long you can make it before you run out of lives.
